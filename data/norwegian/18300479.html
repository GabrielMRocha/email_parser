What are my rights?

If after you have made your Booking we significantly change the departure- or arrival time for your flight, by more than 60 minutes, to a time that is unacceptable for you, and we are unable to obtain a seat on an alternative flight that is acceptable for you, we will provide a refund in accordance with Article 11. In addition to this, the provisions in clause 11.2.3 and clause 16.4 will apply.

This applies if you have not already accepted the change.
Please note that we can only offer to change to the original destination.
